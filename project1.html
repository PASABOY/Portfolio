Street Art

What We Did

My friend Shyheim and I worked together on the scripts for this project. We used free assets we found online to create a small game where you spray paint on walls.

What I Learned and Enjoyed

I learned how to collaborate effectively with a teammate and enjoyed the creative process of designing the gameplay mechanics. It was fun to see our ideas come to life.

What I Didn't Enjoy

The debugging process was challenging and time-consuming, especially when dealing with asset compatibility issues.
