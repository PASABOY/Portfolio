Life on Mars

What I Did

This was a solo project that served as my introduction to Unity. I created the scripts with C# and camera scenes using Cinemachine, and the world was built using pre-made assets provided by my school.

What I Learned and Enjoyed

I learned the basics of Unity and how to use Cinemachine for dynamic camera scenes. I enjoyed exploring Unity's features and seeing my ideas come to life in a 3D environment.

What I Didn't Enjoy

The learning curve was steep, and I faced challenges understanding some of Unity's tools and workflows initially.
